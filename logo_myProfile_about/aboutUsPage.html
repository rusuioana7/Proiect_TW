Statistics

Obesity is a major public health issue in Europe, approximately one in six adults considered obese. According to Eurostat, the EU statistics agency, 15.9% of adults in the EU were classified as obese in 2014, up from 14.9% in 2008. Obesity is associated with health problems: heart disease, diabetes, and types of cancer. Read More
